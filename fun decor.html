♡ Humble Beginnings ♡

I’ve been drawing since I was a kid, but it wasn’t until middle school that I began creating pieces for others.

I fell in love with transforming their ideas into something tangible and adding my own creative spark along the way ♡

The past few years I’ve been lucky enough to work with vtubers and creators from all over the world

♡

helping them bring their characters to life and dreams to reality! Along the way I earned my degree in game development, and worked my butt off through the stress that is university.
